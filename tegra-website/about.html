Our Mission

Empowering high school students in Rwanda to navigate tech careers through mentorship and skill development.

Problem Statement: In Rwanda today, many highschool students want to be data scientist, software engineers or web developers, but many times they don't understand where to start, who to talk to for guidance as these fields are wide and big. A 2022 survey by the Rwanda Education Board showed that 62% of students interested in tech couldnâ€™t name a clear career goal, and only 18% had someone in the tech field to guide them. Even though there are amazing learning opportunities online through platforms like Coursera, Udemy or programs like ALX, fewer than 25% of students knew how to find or use them. Without the right guidance and support, many young people risk wasting time, losing motivation, or giving up on a field they could truly thrive in.
Mission Statement: Our mission is to empower students in highschool who are interested in tech, by helping them choose the right path, build their skills and connect with mentors.
